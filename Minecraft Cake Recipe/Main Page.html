Minecraft Cake Recipe

By Notch

This cake is a great source of nutrition and an exciting dish to have with your friends after a successful boss fight!
You can even put a candle into it for birthdays!

Ingredients

  • Crafting Table
  • 3 Wheat
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 Milk Buckets
  • 2 Sugars

Instructions

  1. Open Crafting Table
  2. Place the ingredients into the crafting table as shown in the image below:
  3. Move the Cake to your Inventory

You now have a tasty cake for you and your friends, Enjoy!
